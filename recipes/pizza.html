Homemade Pizza

Description

A delicious and easy recipe to make for your friends and family using basic ingredients!

This recipe is perfect for beginners and experienced cooks alike. With simple steps you can create a fresh pizza dough that pairs perfectly with your favorite toppings.

Ingredients - 2 servings

  • Wheat Flour - 4 cups
  • Oil - 2 tbsp
  • Salt - 1 tsp
  • Sugar - 1 tsp
  • Warm water
  • Fresh yeast - 2 cubes

Steps

  1. Place the wheat flour in a bowl and add the oil, warm water, and the two cubes of fresh yeast dissolved with the sugar and salt
  2. Stir with a wooden spoon, then knead the dough well with your hands until it no longer sticks to them
  3. Let it rest for approximately 30 minutes
  4. Grease the baking pan with butter and then spread the dough over it
  5. Add the toppings to your liking
  6. Bake in a preheated oven until golden brown, for about 25 to 35 minutes
