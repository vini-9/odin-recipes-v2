Lasagna

Description

The perfect recipe for a Sunday get-together with your friends and family!

Made with fresh ingredients and simple steps, this recipe is perfect for anyone looking for a comforting homemade meal

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles - 12 sheets
  • Ground beef - 500g
  • Tomato sauce - 2 cups
  • Onion - 1 small, chopped
  • Garlic - 2 cloves, minced
  • Olive oil - 2 tbsp
  • Salt - 1 tsp
  • Black pepper - ½ tsp
  • Ricotta cheese - 1 cup
  • Mozzarella cheese - 2 cups, shredded
  • Parmesan cheese - ½ cup, grated
  • Fresh basil - optional

Steps

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions and set aside
  2. In a pan, add the olive oil and sauté the onion and garlic until fragant
  3. Add the ground beef and cook until browned
  4. Season with salt and pepper, add the tomato sauce and stir. Let it simmer for 10 minutes
  5. Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish
  6. Add a layer of noodles, followed by ricotta, more meat sauce, and mozzarella
  7. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used
  8. Finish with a generous layer of mozzarella and parmesan on top
  9. Bake in a preheated oven at 180°C (350°F) for 25–30 minutes, or until golden and bubbling
  10. Let it rest for 5 minutes before serving
