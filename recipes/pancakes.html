Fluffy Homemade Pancakes

Description

Pancakes are a classic breakfast favorite, and this simple recipe makes soft and fluffy pancakes using basic ingredients you probably already have at home.

Ingredients - 4 servings

  • All-purpose flour - 1 cup
  • Sugar - 2 tbsp
  • Baking powder - 1 tbsp
  • Milk - 1 cup
  • Egg - 1 unit
  • Butter - 2 tbsp, melted
  • Vanilla extract - 1 tsp
  • Salt - A pinch

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk together the milk, egg, vanilla, and melted butter.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix gently until combined. Do not overmix.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and lightly grease it.
  5. Pour about Â¼ cup of batter for each pancake.
  6. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown.
  7. Serve warm with syrup, butter, or your favorite toppings.
